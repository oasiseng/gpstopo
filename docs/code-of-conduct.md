# Code of Conduct

GPSTopo is committed to fostering a collaborative, respectful community of
geospatial professionals and open-source contributors.

## Our Standards

Participants agree to:

- Demonstrate professionalism and courtesy in all communications.
- Provide constructive feedback focused on ideas, not individuals.
- Respect privacy by excluding client-identifying or sensitive information from
  public channels.
- Credit the work of others and avoid plagiarism.
- Follow applicable laws, regulations, and site safety requirements.

## Unacceptable Behavior

We will not tolerate:

- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments.
- Sharing confidential client data, coordinates, or financial information.
- Knowingly submitting misleading or falsified data.
- Vandalism of documentation, templates, or project resources.

## Scope

This code applies to all GPSTopo repositories, documentation, events, and
communication platforms. It covers online and in-person interactions, including
field operations where GPSTopo personnel are present.

## Reporting

- Report violations to the project maintainers at `ops@gpstopo.com`.
- Provide context, links, and any supporting evidence so we can investigate.
- Reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

## Enforcement

- Maintainers may remove contributions that violate this code.
- Repeat or severe violations can result in temporary or permanent bans from the
  project community.
- When appropriate, maintainers will coordinate with employers or legal
  authorities for serious incidents.

By participating in GPSTopo projects, you agree to uphold these standards and
contribute to a safe, inclusive environment for everyone.
