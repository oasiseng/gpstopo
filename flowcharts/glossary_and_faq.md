## üìñ Glossary of Terms

### Survey & Mapping Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **ALTA/NSPS Survey** | American Land Title Association survey ‚Äî detailed commercial property survey meeting specific standards for title insurance and lending |
| **As-Built** | Survey documenting the actual constructed positions of buildings, utilities, and site features (vs. design drawings) |
| **Benchmark** | Known elevation point used as reference for other measurements |
| **Boundary Survey** | Mapping showing property lines, corners, and legal boundaries |
| **CAD** | Computer-Aided Design ‚Äî software used for creating technical drawings (e.g., AutoCAD) |
| **Contour Lines** | Lines on a map connecting points of equal elevation |
| **Control Point** | Surveyed reference point with known coordinates |
| **DWG** | AutoCAD drawing file format (editable) |
| **Easement** | Legal right to use another's property for specific purpose (e.g., utility access) |
| **GIS** | Geographic Information System ‚Äî technology for storing and analyzing spatial data |
| **GPS** | Global Positioning System ‚Äî satellite-based positioning technology |
| **LiDAR** | Light Detection and Ranging ‚Äî laser scanning technology for creating 3D terrain models |
| **Orthomosaic** | Geometrically corrected aerial photograph (no distortion) |
| **PDF** | Portable Document Format (non-editable, for viewing/printing) |
| **PE** | Professional Engineer ‚Äî licensed engineer who can certify engineering work |
| **PLS** | Professional Land Surveyor ‚Äî licensed surveyor who can certify boundary and ALTA surveys |
| **Point Cloud** | Collection of data points in 3D space (from LiDAR or photogrammetry) |
| **Staking** | Physical marking of design points in the field for construction |
| **Topographic Survey** | Mapping showing elevations, contours, and terrain features |

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### For Clients

**Q: How fast can I get my survey?**  
A: Standard turnaround is 3-7 days depending on service type. Rush options available (24-72 hours) for additional fee.

**Q: Do I need to be present for the field work?**  
A: Usually not. We just need access to the property. We'll coordinate timing with you.

**Q: What file formats will I receive?**  
A: Standard delivery includes PDF (for viewing/printing) and DWG (for CAD editing). Additional formats available on request.

**Q: Is this a "real" survey?**  
A: Our topographic and boundary maps are prepared by or verified by licensed professionals. For legal boundary determination or ALTA surveys, we use our licensed PLS network. All deliverables include appropriate disclaimers about their intended use.

**Q: What if I need changes after delivery?**  
A: One free revision is included if the issue is within the original scope. Additional changes may incur a fee.

**Q: Can I use this for a building permit?**  
A: Depends on your local jurisdiction. Our topo and boundary maps are commonly accepted for residential permits. Check with your building department or let us know ‚Äî we can provide additional certifications if needed.

---

### For Partners (Field Techs & PLS)

**Q: How do I get assigned to projects?**  
A: Our system auto-assigns based on location, service type, and current workload. You'll receive WhatsApp notification when assigned.

**Q: What's the payment structure?**  
A: You're paid per project at agreed rates. Payment processed within 5 business days of client delivery.

**Q: What if I can't complete on time?**  
A: Notify us ASAP (ideally 24+ hours before deadline). We can extend deadline or reassign if necessary.

**Q: What happens if client requests revision?**  
A: If revision is due to error on our end, we handle it at no cost to you. If it's client scope change, we charge client and may provide additional pay.

**Q: Do I need my own insurance?**  
A: Yes. Licensed professionals must carry E&O insurance. Field techs should have general liability coverage.

---

### For Team Members

**Q: How do we handle rush orders?**  
A: Rush orders get priority assignment (partners with <2 active projects). Increased payout helps incentivize faster turnaround.

**Q: What if a client is unhappy with deliverable?**  
A: First, review against original scope and QC checklist. If legitimate issue, offer free revision. If out-of-scope request, quote additional work. Escalate to founders if needed.

**Q: How do we track project status?**  
A: All projects tracked in Airtable/Trello. Status updates trigger automatic client notifications. Check board daily for any stuck projects.

**Q: What's our refund policy?**  
A: Full refund if requested before field work begins. After work started, case-by-case basis (typically 50% refund or free revision option).

**Q: How do we onboard new partners?**  
A: 1) Verify license/credentials, 2) Add to partner database, 3) Assign test project with extra QC, 4) Review performance before full integration.

### Florida (Our HQ State)

- **Boundary surveys:** Must be performed by or under direct supervision of licensed PLS
- **Topographic surveys:** Can be performed by non-licensed tech if PE-reviewed for engineering use
- **ALTA surveys:** Always require PLS
- **As-builts:** Depends on use case (PLS or PE depending on scope)

### General U.S. Guidelines

Every state has different licensing requirements. When in doubt:
1. Check state surveying board website
2. Use licensed PLS for any work involving property boundaries
3. Include appropriate disclaimers on all deliverables
